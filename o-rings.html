Onion Rings

Fried, "onion-y" goodness! Essential for any burger meal or watch them shine in their own glory as you clear a plate of these onion rings!

Ingredients

  • 4 large sweet onions, peeled and sliced into thick rings
  • 1 cup eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups bread crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons red pepper
  • 1 quart oil for frying

Steps

  1. Place the onions, eggs, and flour into a separate shallow bowl.
  2. In another shallow bowl, stir together the bread crumbs, salt, pepper, cayenne, oregano, basil, and red pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a heavy skillet or deep-fryer to 365 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  4. Dip rings of onion into flour, then into the egg, and then into the bread crumb mixture, shaking off any excess after each dip.
  5. Carefully drop each onion ring into the hot oil, and fry for about 30 seconds on each side, or until golden.
  6. Remove from hot oil to paper towels to drain.
