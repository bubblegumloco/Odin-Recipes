Ingredients

Creating The Patty

  • 1 Pound Crab Meat
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons minced chives
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Hot sauce/Tabasco sauce to taste
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons unsalted butter

Assembling The Patty

  • Hamburger Buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • American Cheese
  • Dill Pickle Slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion Ring

Steps

  1. Mix crab, egg, breadcrumbs, mayonnaise, chives, mustard, lemon juice, celery seed, onion powder, pepper and hot sauce in a large bowl.
  2. Form into 6 patties.
  3. Heat oil and butter in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until the butter stops foaming.
  4. Cook the patties until golden brown, about 4 minutes per side.
  5. Add cheese on top of the patty to melt. (Optional)

Put The Pieces together

  • Bottom of the Hamburger Bun
  • Patty
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Cheese (if you prefer not to melt the cheese)
  • Two Pickle Slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion Ring
  • Top of the Hamburger Bun
